> **Document:** The 6 pillars, content formats, weekly calendar, and success metrics.

## 1. The 6 Content Pillars

Every post belongs to exactly ONE pillar:

| Pillar | Code | Purpose | % of Posts | Subscriber Feeling |
|--------|:----:|---------|:----------:|-------------------|
| **Accent Lesson** | 🎯 AL | Teach a specific sound/pattern | 30% | "I learned something real" |
| **Social Proof** | 🔥 SP | Student progress, results, milestones | 20% | "Others like me are succeeding" |
| **Myth Destroyer** | 💣 MD | Kill a common English-learning belief | 15% | "I've been doing it wrong" |
| **System Reveal** | 🏛️ SR | Explain how EEC works | 15% | "These people are serious" |
| **Brand Story** | 👑 BS | Vision, philosophy, MACAL identity | 10% | "I want to be part of this" |
| **Invitation** | 📢 IN | Clear CTA — take assessment, join | 10% | "I should act now" |

## 2. Content Formats

| Format | Description | Best For Pillar | Length |
|--------|-------------|:---------------:|:------:|
| **Voice Note + Caption** | 30-90s audio + short text | AL | 60s + 5 lines |
| **Text Post (Short)** | 3-8 lines, one idea | MD, SP | 50-100 words |
| **Text Post (Long)** | Structured with bullets/headers | SR, BS | 150-300 words |
| **Image + Caption** | Branded graphic + explanation | AL (Word of Day) | Image + 3-5 lines |
| **Audio Comparison** | Two recordings (wrong vs right, or Day 1 vs 30) | SP, AL | 2 clips + 4 lines |
| **Poll** | Simple question + 3-4 options | Engagement | 1 question |
| **Button Post** | Text + inline button (link) | IN | 4-6 lines + button |

## 8. Content Rules

1. **80/20 Rule:** 80% gives value with zero ask. 20% invites action.
2. **"Would I forward this?" Test:** If a subscriber wouldn't forward it to a friend learning English — rewrite or don't post.
3. **3-Second Rule:** First line must hook. Lead with insight, not context.
4. **Voice Notes Are Gold:** Product demo. Proof of expertise. Use weekly.
5. **Never Post "Just to Post":** One excellent post > three mediocre ones.
6. **Silent CTA:** Even value posts end with a breadcrumb toward the system.
7. **Pinned = Funnel:** Pinned message is always the "what is EEC + how to join" entry point.
8. **Recycle Hits:** Repost top-performing evergreen content every 6-8 weeks.
