## Design Rationale

- **"أهلًا بيك"** not "أهلا وسهلا بكم" — masri, warm, direct
- **Three sections (✅ ❌ ⚡)** — scannable, clear boundaries
- **"مفيش سؤال غبي"** — removes fear of asking (biggest barrier for beginners)
- **"كل سؤال بتسأله ممكن يفيد ١٠ ناس"** — reframes asking as contribution
- **Ban policy is clear and immediate** — no warnings for spam/ads (protects community quality)
- **Bot CTA at the end** — subtle funnel (not aggressive)
- **Voice note limit (2 min)** — prevents group from becoming a voice dump
- **Arabic + English allowed** — no language policing in the GROUP (that's for Discord levels)

---

## Moderation Notes

- Spam: Delete + ban immediately (no warning)
- Off-topic: Delete message, send reminder (1st time). Mute 24h (2nd time).
- Genuine questions: Never delete. Even if "basic" — beginners need to feel safe.
- Self-promotion: Delete. If subtle, warn once. If repeated, ban.
- The bot will auto-seed this group with prompts and follow-ups (see 06-AUTOMATION-ENGINE.md)
