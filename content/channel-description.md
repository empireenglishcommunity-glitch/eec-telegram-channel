## Design Rationale

- **"نظام" not "كورس"** — positions EEC as different from every other English course
- **"American Accent من اليوم الأول"** — the core differentiator stated immediately
- **"مش course. ده نظام بيخليك تتكلم"** — destroys the main objection (another course?) in one line
- **🏛️ emoji** — brand mark (empire/pillars)
- **No fluff, no promises** — just a fact about what this is
