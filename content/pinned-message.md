## Design Rationale

### Structure Logic:
1. **Brand + one-line hook** — immediate identity
2. **"إيه ده؟"** — answers "what is this channel?" with bullet points (scannable)
3. **"ليه تفضل هنا؟"** — gives reasons to stay subscribed (daily value)
4. **"تعمل إيه دلوقتي؟"** — 3 clear CTAs with links (funnel entry points)
5. **Posting schedule** — sets expectations (rhythm = trust)
6. **Brand signature** — MACAL mark

### Funnel Design:
- CTA #1 (Assessment) — low friction, free, impressive → builds trust
- CTA #2 (Bot) — for people ready to buy/learn more
- CTA #3 (Discord) — for people ready to commit NOW

### Why This Works:
- Scannable in 10 seconds (bold headers, bullets, short lines)
- Three clear "next steps" — subscriber always knows what to do
- Shows DAILY value (they know what to expect from the channel)
- No hard sell — just "here's what we offer, here's where to go"
- Arabic throughout — feels native, not translated
